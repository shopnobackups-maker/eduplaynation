BRAVE games are a Game Theory application, K4-5 longform board game history games that study the role trust plays in conflict & cooperation. Whereby a classroom is divided into 5 or 6 teams and tasked with identifying their competitive advantage so they can buy, sell, and trade unique game piecesnatural resources, iron tools, commodities, livestock, land, gold, and silverdepending on the region and era. Gameplay becomes collaborative as student-led teams realize everyone wins more when everyone wins.
